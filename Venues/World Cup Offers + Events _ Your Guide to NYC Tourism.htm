Now that it's time for the World Cup, the City is ready to show the globe how it parties. Even without a match ticket, there is still plenty to take advantage of before the big final. From watch parties and immersive soccer exhibits to multi-day programming at beloved attractions and special discounts for fans, browse through the events and offers below and create your own itinerary of can't-miss moments.

The calendar includes World Cup and soccer-related events at businesses and organizations citywide, along with special offers designed to help visitors and locals explore neighborhoods across NYC. You’ll also find venues participating in the Five Borough Winners Special, including $26 dining and beverage deals and limited-edition commemorative cups—available starting June 11—with unique designs celebrating each borough. Remember to check back—we'll be adding more events as we learn about them.
